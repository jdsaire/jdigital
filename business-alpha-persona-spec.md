## Identity anchor *(source of truth: T1 — do not paraphrase loosely)*
- **One-liner:** A behavioral product architect who turns innovation strategy into digital products people actually adopt — and builds them himself.
- **Differentiator:** continuity of ownership across the gap that normally requires three handoffs — boardroom intent → behavior-grounded design → production code he writes → program discipline that lands it. Maps to the site spine **CREA / CRECE / CONTROLA** (BUILD / SCALE / CONTROL).
- **Problem-frame he resolves:** innovation stalls between the strategy deck and a shipped product — scopers can't build, builders ignore adoption, no one owns the path.

## Voice parameters
1. **Posture — magnet, not megaphone.** Operates from abundance; filters, never chases. Velvet-rope selectivity is the business translation of the archetype's "I set traps, I filter, I dictate the terms."
2. **Register (Rule C2).** First person by default for work / capability / action. *JDigital* as actor only for standards and manifesto. *El estratega / activo ejecutivo* only inside the services offer. Bridges stay hybrid — photo + verbatim signal the principal is speaking.
3. **Sentence shape.** Short, declarative. Dominant pattern = problem statement + ownership claim ("La fricción destruye el valor comercial. Audito el recorrido…").
4. **Authority without boast.** Confidence carried by verbs and shipped outcomes — never superlatives ("absoluto" was cut). Zero validation: challenge the reader.
5. **No hedging, no justifying, no explaining.** Assertive declaratives only.
6. **Time as premium.** Evaluation framing, not solicitation (48h hábiles; "determinará si la intervención es viable").
7. **Formal *usted*.** Tech-fluent anglicism mix kept deliberate (C4).
8. **Lexical core.** Nouns: ejecución, despliegue, intervención, arsenal, estándar, Triple Cero. Verbs: inspeccionar, asegurar, dictar, auditar, ejecutar.

## Forbidden patterns
- Begging / soliciting: *contáctanos, no dudes en, estaremos encantados,* free-quote framing.
- Boastful superlatives: *absoluto, el mejor, líder indiscutible,* "the one person who can."
- Hedging: *quizás, podríamos, intentamos.*
- Generic consulting filler: *soluciones a medida, tu socio estratégico,* "developer for hire."
- Team / agency voice (*nosotros*) — he is a principal, not a firm — except *JDigital*-as-standard.
- Any trace of the archetype's physical / lifestyle / sexual register.
- Emoji.

## Conversion-funnel tone
- **Hero + bridges (magnet):** assert scale and standard; the principal speaks.
- **Work + capabilities (proof):** first-person executed outcomes; numbers and shipped truth, not adjectives.
- **Manifesto / Triple Cero (filter):** non-negotiable terms that repel low-fit leads — the velvet rope.
- **Contact (evaluation):** application, not purchase; viability + 48h hábiles.

## Hard factual DO-NOTs *(from T1 — non-negotiable)*
- Never use **"12-project portfolio"** — contradicts shipped truth.
- Frame **TUUA** as operational standard-setting, not the detached audit.
